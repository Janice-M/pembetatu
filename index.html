Pembetatu

Pembetatu is a web application that helps users figure out the names of different types of triangles after they enter various lengths into the input boxes and submit.

Below is a summary explanation of the possible results:

  • A scalene triangle is a triangle that has three unequal sides
  • An equilateral triangle is a triangle in which all three sides are equal
  • An isosceles triangle is a triangle that has two sides of equal length
